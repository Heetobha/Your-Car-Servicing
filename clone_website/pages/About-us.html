About Victory Auto Service

Family Owned And Operated Since 1997

Owners Jeff & Debby Matt

Jeff Matt founded Victory Auto Service & Glass with a commitment to providing exceptional service to those in need of automotive repair and maintenance. Growing up in Minnesota, Jeff worked as an automotive technician before opening Victory Auto Service & Glass. Jeff had always dreamed of opening a shop where the customer always felt cared for, never felt taken advantage of, and always received the highest quality service at the best price.

Victory Auto Service & Glass first opened our doors in 1997 with a small, one-bay shop in Spring Lake Park. We have been growing ever since, thanks to our loyal and supportive customers like you.

We Believe in Paying it Forward

When we open an auto repair shop, we join a community. At all of our locations we believe in participating in actively serving those in community with us. A primary way we serve others in our community is though exceptional auto repair services at straight-forward, competitive prices you deserve. Also serving our community can mean partnering with organizations who are in our community and are doing good every day.
